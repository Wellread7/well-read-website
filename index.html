Powerful Reading Features

Well Read transforms the way you consume written content with innovative reading modes and powerful tools.

RSVP Reading

Speed through text with our Rapid Serial Visual Presentation technology. See past words below the current one and tap any to navigate back.

Scan Mode

A unique reading mode that guides your eyes by highlighting the current word whilst keeping other text opaque.

Content Import

Upload files, parse webpages via share extension, scan documents, and upload pictures for instant reading.

In-App Browser

Read any webpage directly in the app with our built-in browser. Use RSVP reading on any website without leaving the app.

Stats & Insights

Get detailed analytics about your reading habits, speed improvements, and comprehension levels over time.

Highlighting & Notes

Highlight any text, add notes to your highlights, and easily access them later with just a tap.

Word Definitions

Long press any word to instantly get its definition, enhancing your vocabulary as you read.

Text Customisation

Completely personalise your reading experience with custom text colours, backgrounds, fonts (including OpenDyslexic), size, and spacing.

AI-Powered Intelligence

Well Read leverages artificial intelligence to enhance your reading experience and help you learn more effectively.

Smart Summaries

Generate customised summaries based on your content type - whether you're reading fiction, non-fiction, technical documents, news articles, academic papers, or educational materials.

Reading Recap

Never lose track of your reading progress. When returning to a book after a break, the AI provides a personalised recap of what you've already read, helping you quickly refresh your memory before continuing.

Quiz Generation

Create custom quizzes from any text you're reading. Choose the number of questions and test your comprehension with automatically generated multiple-choice questions.

Track Learning Progress

Keep track of your quiz scores and comprehension metrics to see how well you understand different reading materials over time.
